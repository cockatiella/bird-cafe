Cockatiels

Cockatiels are native to Australia, and favour the Australian wetlands, scrublands, and bush lands.

Cockatoo

Cockatoos are recognisable by the showy crests and curved bills. Their plumage is generally less colourful than that of other parrots, being mainly white, grey, or black and often with coloured features in the crest, cheeks, or tail.

Sun Conure

Sun Conures are native to South America. They are found in the wild in northeast Brazil, Guyana, Paraguay, Bolivia, and north of the Amazon River in Brazil.

Quaker

Quaker parrots are native to South America. It is a small, bright-green parrot with a greyish breast and greenish-yellow abdomen. Its average lifespan is 20–30 years. It originates from the temperate to subtropical areas of South America.

Indian Ringneck

The Indian ringneck parakeet is a medium-sized parrot that measures between 14 and 17 inches in length at maturity. It is a slender bird, with a long tail and a small but powerful beak. The plumage of the Indian ringneck parakeet is usually a shade of green, with a distinctive blue tail and yellow under-feathers.

Lovebirds

Lovebirds are native to Africa and the island of Madagascar. They are small parrots that are named for the strong pair bonds they form with their chosen mate. Lovebirds are a popular pet bird, but their small size and active nature can make them a challenging pet for many people.
